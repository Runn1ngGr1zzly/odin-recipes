Delicious Spinach and Turkey Lasagna

Description

A delicious version of lasagna for those of us who are health conscious.

Ingredients

  • 9 whole-wheat lasagna noodles
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • Half cup chopped onion
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 3 cups tomatoe sauce
  • .5 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 3 tablespoons italian seasoning
  • .25 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 cups chopped fresh spinach
  • 2 cups fat-free ricotta cheese
  • .25 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
